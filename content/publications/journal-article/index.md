abstract: "This study develops a computational framework for predicting stock price movements of Bank Negara Indonesia (BBNI) using the Exponential Moving Average (EMA) Crossover method. By leveraging dynamic historical stock data, the system evaluates structural price trends through a combination of short-term (EMA 10) and long-term (EMA 20) exponential indicators. The algorithmic approach tracks momentum changes to trigger automated buy signals (Golden Cross) and sell signals (Death Cross), reducing operational latency in volatile financial environments. Experimental evaluations demonstrate that the system serves as a highly robust back-end decision support engine for technical traders, delivering stable execution and systematic risk mitigation through algorithmic market logic."

### Additional Research Information
This software framework implements computational tracking of historical stock data using Python libraries, including Pandas for structured data manipulation and Matplotlib for automated charting generation. The core backend engine processes moving average equations to map golden crosses and death crosses into concrete, programmatic execution rules for high-performance financial systems.
